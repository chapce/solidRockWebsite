About Us

Our Story & Mission

A Church Built on Faith and Community

Solid Rock Community Church was founded in 1992 by Rev Dr. Rudy Morgan with a simple vision: to create a place where people could encounter God's love and grow in their faith together.

Throughout our history, we've remained committed to preaching Biblical truth, fostering authentic relationships, and serving our community with compassion.

We believe the church is more than a building — it is a family united by God's love.

Our Core Beliefs

We believe in one God — the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

We believe the Bible contains all truth necessary for faith and Christian living.

We believe humanity is born with a fallen nature and needs redemption.

We believe salvation comes through Jesus Christ.

We believe believers are called to live holy lives through faith.

We believe the Holy Spirit guides and transforms believers.

We believe Christ will return and final judgment will occur.

Rev. Terrence Chapman

Pastor Terrence Chapman serves in the Church of the Nazarene with a passion for ministering to people of all ages. He lives in Baldwin, New York with his wife Cordell and their four children.

In addition to ministry, he has spent over 30 years in education as a high school mathematics and computer science teacher and instructional coach.

Pastor Terrence warmly welcomes anyone who wishes to connect with the Solid Rock church family.
