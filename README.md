# solidRockWebsite
For my midterm project in BCS 350, I chose the non-profit organization of Solid Rock Church of the Nazarene to create a website for. It will showcase the churches presence in the community, stating the mission, whilst giving direct ways to find the churches scheduled events
This is the link to my figma design: https://www.figma.com/make/1BhDHiJ4fK9d7Fd920IUDU/Church-website-design?t=m21vLqa3TfS0O1Ri-6


1. What is the name of your business?
Solid Rock Church of the Nazarene

2. Is the business real or fictional?
real

3. What products or services does the business offer?
Worship services, Community Outreach events, Space to host for weddings, and other events. Specific ministry and programs

4. Who is the target audience?
People seeking community, and a place to grow spiritually. 

5. What problem does this business solve? 
Social isolation, creating a space for community, and charity/ giving back to the community. 

6. What makes this business unique compared to competitors?
Strong Pillar in the community for years, and welcoming to a wide range of cultural backgrounds.

7. What pages will your website include (e.g., Home, About, Services, Contact)?
Home, About, Zoom links to church, bible study, etc., listing different missions, like 

8. Write a short mission statement for the business.
To make christlike disciples both near and far.

9. What tone should the website content have (professional, friendly, modern, etc.)? Friendly and also modern. 

10. What call-to-action do you want visitors to take?

I would like visitors to learn more, and want to view or seek to visit a church service. 
11. What information must be immediately visible on the homepage?
The name of the church, the persons involved 

12. Will the site include images, videos, or other media? Why?
The site will include images, and links to online media.

PART 2: DESIGN (Answer ALL questions, add your own if necessary)

1. What overall style will the website have (minimalist, corporate, creative, etc.)?
Creative, and also modern.
2. What color scheme will you use and why? 
Burgundy will be the main color because it is the representative color of the church. Tan as well because it complements this color. 
3. What fonts will you use for headings and body text?
Sans-serif fonts; Inter, Righteous.
4. How will spacing and layout improve readability?
Spacing and layout improve readability because there it creates faster navigation and understanding for the user. 
6. What visual hierarchy will guide the user’s attention?
The largest print text will grab attention first, as well as animations, and brighter colors.
7. How will consistency be maintained across pages?
The color scheme will be varied across the pages. 
8. What icons will you use?
I will use the church of the nazarene icon in the upper left corner(placeholder is currently a cross). Mail, facebook, instagram and youtube logos. 
9. What emotions should the design evoke in users?
A sincere and friendly, and passionate emotion should be invoked in users. 
10. How will accessibility be considered (contrast, font size, etc.)?
The simplicity of navigation of the website
11. What inspiration sources influenced your design?
Other church websites, the color schemes used, and the way they display photos. 
12. Describe the layout of the homepage and build the wireframe
The layout ensures the user reads the church statement and name first, and holds them as the forefront. Links and buttons will lead to more information.
